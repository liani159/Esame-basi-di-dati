How it work

here is the main page

here you have the searching section for books

if you submit witouht inserting nothing, it will show you
all the book in the database

here you can insert the complete or not complete title of a book
and it will show you the result: the book(s)
starting with the giving string

here you can print all the books write by a specific author by submitting, only his complete name

you can also submit by entying both the title and the authors name as long
that the entered name is trully the author of the title book inserted

by submitting a complete or incomplete name of authors, you can print
all information about him/her of all authors with a name starting by the string entered

here is the part reserved agli utenti

if you've clicked on inserire studente button
you'll have to registrate a new student, it will show a succesfully
inserted if all is gone correctly.

if you've clicked on ricerca studente button
you'll bring on this section. if you submit witouht write a name it will print
you all the registrated students and the possibility to delete them.

otherwise if you wrote a complete student name it will show you
all informations about him and also the choice to delete him or not.

here is the part reserved to the rent

you've clicked on Prendere in prestito button, and here you have to insert
all the require informations and submit it to rent a book
you'll have a confirmation message.

here if you write "*" in the matrola input field, you will be print all
the rent make , and you'll be able to delete them or not.

here you can write the registration number on a specifi student to show
his historical rent

here you can insert a range of date to print all the rent make during this period.
ps: you can also insert a particular reg number to show all his rent during the range selected.

Statisctical part, just insert a valid date and submit to print all
